Speaking at Momentum: Tech Edition, a GDG on Campus event hosted by MCKV Institute of Engineering, Howrah. This edition of Momentum is centered around **Build With AI** — exploring how developers can build practical AI-powered applications using Gemini and related tools.

The event brings together students, developers, and practitioners for hands-on learning around AI, model integration, and real-world use cases. I'll be joining as one of the speakers alongside Anubhab Mukherjee to share practical approaches to building with AI, going beyond the demo to discuss what actually works when you ship AI features.

Whether you're experimenting with Gemini for the first time or figuring out how to integrate AI into production workflows, this session is designed to give you actionable takeaways you can apply immediately.
