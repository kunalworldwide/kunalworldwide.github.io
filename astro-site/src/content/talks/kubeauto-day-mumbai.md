Organizer and speaker at KubeAuto Day Mumbai, an event focused on AI and automation in the Kubernetes ecosystem.

KubeAuto Day brings together practitioners, maintainers, and vendors to share knowledge and best practices around intelligent infrastructure automation. From AI-driven cluster optimization to automated workload placement and autonomous remediation, the event explores how artificial intelligence is reshaping how we operate Kubernetes at scale.

As an organizer alongside Viktorija Pajarskė and Vytautas Klova from KubeAuto, I helped curate a lineup of engineers and practitioners working at the intersection of AI agents, cloud infrastructure, and real-time data — creating a space where the conversations between talks are half the reason to show up.
