Co-presented with Gaurang Singh (Field CTO - APAC, CAST AI) at Agentic AI Unplugged Bengaluru, a full-day community meetup focused on AI agents as they exist in practice: tool calling, orchestration, failure modes, and the infrastructure that holds it all together.

Everyone's talking about AI agents. Most of the conversation is hype decks and Twitter threads. This event brought engineers into a room to show what they've actually built, what broke, and what they learned.

This talk explored how a unified platform approach can replace the fragmented sprawl of AI tools that teams accumulate — from model serving to vector databases to agent orchestration — and why consolidation matters when you're operating at scale.
